Support & Help

🧾 How do I upload a lease agreement?

Landlords can go to the “Upload Agreement” section on their dashboard and upload a PDF file.

💳 How can I pay my rent?

Tenants can visit “Pay Rent” on the tenant dashboard to view due amount and mark payment.

🔑 I forgot my password. What do I do?

Use the “Forgot Password” link on the login page (if implemented), or contact support.

📃 What file formats are accepted for lease uploads?

Only PDF files are currently supported. Max size: 5MB.
