Pondicherry (officially known as Puducherry) is a charming coastal town on the southeastern coast of India, blending French colonial heritage with rich Tamil culture. Known for its serene beaches, cobblestone streets, and spiritual retreats, Pondicherry offers a unique blend of history, spirituality, and natural beauty.
